Muhammad Arsh Alfatih Ibn Zubair

Founder & CEO, ALFATIH SOLUTIONS

Muhammad Arsh Alfatih Ibn Zubair is a unique visionary who seamlessly bridges the worlds of Islamic scholarship and modern technology. As an Alim-e-Deen (Islamic Scholar) with deep expertise in artificial intelligence, he represents a new generation of leaders who understand that faith and innovation can work hand in hand.

With fluency in both Arabic and English, Arsh delivers powerful motivational speeches at schools, colleges, and business events, inspiring young minds to embrace technology while staying true to their values. His presentations blend Islamic wisdom with practical insights on AI, business growth, and personal development.

As the founder of ALFATIH SOLUTIONS, Arsh has built a company that reflects his core beliefs: technology should serve humanity with purpose, businesses should grow ethically, and the Muslim community should be at the forefront of technological advancement.
